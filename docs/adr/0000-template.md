## Context

What's the problem? What constraints are in play? Why does this
decision need to be made now?

Keep this section short. State the forces, not the decision.

## Decision

The chosen approach. One paragraph is usually enough. Reference the
modules / files affected.

## Alternatives considered

List the other plausible options. For each, explain in 1–3 sentences
what it would have looked like and why we didn't pick it. Be fair to
the alternatives — they often become relevant again later.

## Consequences

The good, bad, and neutral effects of this decision. Be honest about
tradeoffs we're accepting.

- **Positive:** what gets better.
- **Negative:** what gets worse or stays awkward.
- **Neutral:** observations about how the decision plays out.

## References

- Links to related ADRs, code, external docs, or discussions that
  informed this decision.
